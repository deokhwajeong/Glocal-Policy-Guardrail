# Official Regulatory Sources by Country

This document lists official government and regulatory bodies that publish content compliance rules. These sources can be monitored for automatic policy updates.

---

## South Korea (대한민국)

### Broadcasting & Content Rating
- **방송통신심의위원회 (Korea Communications Standards Commission)**
  - URL: https://www.kocsc.or.kr
  - API: Not publicly available
  - Update Method: Web scraping, RSS feeds
  - Regulations: Broadcasting content standards, online content moderation

- **영상물등급위원회 (Korea Media Rating Board - KMRB)**
  - URL: https://www.kmrb.or.kr
  - API: Not publicly available
  - Regulations: Film and video content ratings (전체, 12세, 15세, 청불)

### Data Protection & Privacy
- **개인정보보호위원회 (Personal Information Protection Commission)**
  - URL: https://www.pipc.go.kr
  - Legal Basis: 개인정보보호법 (Personal Information Protection Act)
  - Update Method: Legal bulletin monitoring

### Youth Protection
- **여성가족부 (Ministry of Gender Equality and Family)**
  - URL: https://www.mogef.go.kr
  - Regulations: 청소년보호법 (Youth Protection Act)
  - Time restrictions: 07:00-22:00 (youth protection hours for certain content)

## United States

### Content Rating
- **Motion Picture Association (MPA)**
  - URL: https://www.motionpictures.org
  - Rating System: G, PG, PG-13, R, NC-17
  - API: Not publicly available
  - Update Method: Industry guidelines monitoring

- **ESRB (Entertainment Software Rating Board)**
  - URL: https://www.esrb.org
  - Rating System: E, E10+, T, M, AO
  - Applicable: Video games, interactive content

### Federal Regulations
- **Federal Communications Commission (FCC)**
  - URL: https://www.fcc.gov
  - API: https://www.fcc.gov/reports-research/developers
  - Regulations: Broadcasting decency standards, advertising restrictions

- **Federal Trade Commission (FTC)**
  - URL: https://www.ftc.gov
  - Regulations: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  - API: https://api.data.gov (limited access)

### State-Level (Gambling)
- **American Gaming Association**
  - URL: https://www.americangaming.org
  - State-by-state gambling laws database
  - Update Method: Legal research services

## Saudi Arabia (المملكة العربية السعودية)

### Media Regulation

  - URL: https://www.gcam.gov.sa
  - Arabic: الهيئة العامة للإعلام المرئي والمسموع

### Communications & IT

  - URL: https://www.cst.gov.sa
  - Regulations: Online content, cybersecurity
  - Update Method: Regulatory bulletins

## Germany

### Content Rating
- **Freiwillige Selbstkontrolle der Filmwirtschaft (FSK)**
  - URL: https://www.fsk.de
  - Rating System: ab 0, ab 6, ab 12, ab 16, ab 18
  - Update Method: Rating database queries

### Youth Protection
- **Kommission für Jugendmedienschutz (KJM)**
  - URL: https://www.kjm-online.de
  - Legal Basis: Jugendschutzgesetz (JuSchG)
  - Regulations: Youth protection, time-based broadcasting restrictions

### Data Protection
- **Bundesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz (BfDI)**
  - URL: https://www.bfdi.bund.de
  - Legal Basis: GDPR implementation in Germany
  - Update Method: Legal bulletins, GDPR updates

### Hate Speech & Illegal Content
- **Bundesnetzagentur (Federal Network Agency)**
  - URL: https://www.bundesnetzagentur.de
  - Legal Basis: Netzwerkdurchsetzungsgesetz (NetzDG)
  - Requirement: 24-hour takedown for illegal content
  - API: Complaint submission system

## China (中国)

### Media Regulation
- **国家广播电视总局 (National Radio and Television Administration - NRTA)**
  - URL: http://www.nrta.gov.cn
  - Former: SARFT (State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television)
  - Regulations: Content pre-approval, censorship guidelines
  - Update Method: Official announcements (Chinese only)

### Cyberspace Administration
- **国家互联网信息办公室 (Cyberspace Administration of China - CAC)**
  - URL: http://www.cac.gov.cn
  - Regulations: Internet content management, cybersecurity law
  - Legal Basis: 网络安全法 (Cybersecurity Law)

### Content Approval
- **中国电影审查委员会 (China Film Administration)**
  - URL: Part of NRTA
  - Requirement: Mandatory pre-approval for all film/video content
  - Update Method: Policy directives, censorship guidelines

## Spain

### Gambling Regulation
- **Dirección General de Ordenación del Juego (DGOJ)**
  - URL: https://www.ordenacionjuego.es
  - Regulations: Spanish Gambling Act
  - Time Restrictions: Gambling ads allowed 01:00-05:00 only
  - API: Licensing database

### Audiovisual Content
- **Instituto de la Cinematografía y de las Artes Audiovisuales (ICAA)**
  - URL: https://www.culturaydeporte.gob.es/cultura/areas/cine/
  - Rating System: ICAA classifications
  - Update Method: Official bulletins

### Data Protection
- **Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD)**
  - URL: https://www.aepd.es
  - Legal Basis: GDPR, LOPDGDD
  - Update Method: Legal updates, GDPR amendments

## India

### Content Regulation
- **Ministry of Information and Broadcasting**
  - URL: https://www.mib.gov.in
  - Regulations: IT Rules 2021, OTT platform guidelines
  - Requirement: Grievance officer appointment, parental controls

### Film Certification
- **Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC)**
  - URL: https://www.cbfcindia.gov.in
  - Rating System: U, U/A, A, S
  - Update Method: Certification guidelines monitoring

### Gaming & Gambling
- **State-level regulations**
  - Permitted: Rummy, Fantasy Sports (skill-based)
  - Prohibited: Casinos (except Goa, Sikkim)
  - Update Method: State legislative monitoring

## Japan

### Content Rating
- **映画倫理機構 (Eirin - Film Classification and Rating Organization)**
  - URL: https://www.eirin.jp
  - Rating System: G, PG-12, R15+, R18+
  - Update Method: Industry self-regulation guidelines

### Data Protection
- **個人情報保護委員会 (Personal Information Protection Commission)**
  - URL: https://www.ppc.go.jp
  - Legal Basis: 個人情報保護法 (Act on Protection of Personal Information)
  - API: Not publicly available

### Broadcasting
- **総務省 (Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications - MIC)**
  - URL: https://www.soumu.go.jp
  - Regulations: Broadcasting Law
  - Update Method: Ministerial ordinances

## European Union (Cross-Border)

### Data Protection
- **European Data Protection Board (EDPB)**
  - URL: https://edpb.europa.eu
  - Legal Basis: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  - API: Legal documents database
  - Update Method: Official decisions, guidelines

### Audiovisual Media Services
- **European Audiovisual Observatory**
  - URL: https://www.obs.coe.int
  - Database: MAVISE (TV and on-demand audiovisual services)
  - API: Available for members
  - Coverage: 42 European countries

## Automated Update Strategies

### 2. RSS/Atom Feeds

- Examples: FCC News, AEPD Updates, EDPB Decisions

### 3. Legal Research APIs

- **LexisNexis API**: Global legal database
  - URL: https://www.lexisnexis.com/api

  - Coverage: 200+ countries

- **Thomson Reuters Westlaw**: Legal research API
  - Coverage: International regulations
  - Real-time legal updates

- **Bloomberg Law API**: Regulatory tracking
  - Focus: Financial and media regulations

- **USA**: https://api.data.gov

- **UK**: https://data.gov.uk
